About Us

We are the Leading Real Estate Company.

We are a real estate brokerage, development, and investment advisory firm domiciled in Ikoyi, the most high-brow neighborhood in Lagos, Nigeria, with highly experienced affiliates in neighboring cities. At Centania homes, we see a home as the most valuable investment, and the greatest necessity in the world of man.

We believe that every income earner, irrespective of their varying budgets, should own a home which meets the increasing demand of the modern-day urban architecture – a home embedded in luxury. Consequently, we make the process of buying, selling, and rehabilitating homes for our clients hassle-free.
